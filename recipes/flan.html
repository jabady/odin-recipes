Flan Recipe

Description

A custard dessert with a layer of caramel. A recipe my family loves to make.

It is not a difficult recipe to make, the only complicated part is making the caramel and the most complicated tool to have is a blender.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 14 ounce can of sweetend condensed milk
  • 8 ounces of softened cream cheese
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 12 ounce can of evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Use a blender to combine condensed milk, evaporated milk, cream cheese, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Blend until smooth, should take about 30 seconds.
  4. Set aside.
  5. Set a roasting pan filled half way with water in over.
  6. Heat sugar and water at a medium heat in a small saucepan.
  7. It will take about 8 to 10 minutes to create caramel from sugar and water.
  8. NEVER STOP STIRRING
  9. Pour melted sugar caramel (heated sugar water mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish coating the bottom and sides of the dish.
  10. Pour in the blended dairy mixture into the baking dish.
  11. Place the entire baking dish into the roasting pan filled halfway with water.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes to an hour.
  13. Test for doneness by sticking a knife into the center of the flan. If it comes out clean, you are ready to take it out.
  14. ENJOY FLAN!
