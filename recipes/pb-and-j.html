PBNJ Recipe

Description

A simple childhood classic. Two slices of bread, one slathered with peanut butter and the other slathered with jelly on one side and then combined.

This recipe will now be passed onto you through the ages never to be forgotten.

Modifying this recipe is easy to do and in fact recommended. Often you see honey used as the sweetener, and there are substitutes for peanut butter that can be savory and delicious as well!

Ingredient list

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp of jelly (be careful here if you add too much it can get TOO sweet.

Directions

  1. Grab a butter knife and 2 slices of bread.
  2. Use butter knife to acquire peanut butter and spread to one side of bread.
  3. Grab the other slice of bread without peanut butter and use a butter knife to spread jelly onto it.
  4. Combine both slices of bread togeth, peanut butter and jelly sides facing each other.
  5. Enjoy!
